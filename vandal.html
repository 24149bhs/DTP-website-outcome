Community Favorite Skins

#Rank Skins
#1

Gaia's Vengeance Vandal

About

Gaia's Vengeance Vandal has a unique nature-inspired design, featuring wood textures and glowing vines. Its standout feature is the finisher, where a tree grows from defeated enemies. This creative theme makes it one of the most visually interesting skins in the game.

#2

Kuronami Vandal

About

The Kuronami Vandal is appreciated for its clean and minimalistic aesthetic, featuring a smooth white-and-blue design that feels modern and refined. Unlike more flashy skins, it focuses on simplicity and clarity, which many players find helps maintain focus during matches. Its lightweight feel and subtle effects make it a rising favorite in the community.

#3

Primordium Vandal

About

The Primordium Vandal features a raw, ancient, and fiery aesthetic that feels almost otherworldly. It has strong visual effects like heat buildup and glowing energy, along with a dramatic finisher where a demonic entity drags enemies into another dimension, making it one of the most intense and unique skins in the game.

#4

Prelude to Chaos Vandal

About

Prelude to Chaos Vandal stands out with its intense, dark energy theme and heavy visual effects. The gun feels aggressive and powerful due to its deep, impactful sound design. It's especially popular among players who enjoy dramatic and bold skins.

#5

RGX 11z Pro Vandal

About

The RGX 11z Pro Vandal is a futuristic, gaming-inspired skin with RGB lighting that can change colors during matches. Its sleek design and minimal distractions make it very popular among competitive players. The clean sound and modern aesthetic give it a high-tech feel.

#6

Araxys Vandal

About

The Araxys Vandal features a sci-fi, alien-inspired design with sharp edges and glowing elements. Its unique inspect animations and futuristic sounds make it feel very different from other skins. It's perfect for players who enjoy bold and unusual designs.

#7

Reaver Vandal

About

The Reaver Vandal is known for its dark, gothic aesthetic and glowing purple effects. Its animations feel smooth and powerful, and the deep, heavy shooting sound gives players a strong sense of impact. Many players believe it improves their aim simply because of how satisfying it feels to use

#8

Forsaken Vandal

About

The Forsaken Vandal combines both light and dark aesthetics, offering different color variants like green and gold. It shares a similar feel to the Prime Vandal but with a more detailed and stylish appearance. Players like it for its balance between simplicity and flair.

#9

Prime Vandal

About

The Prime Vandal is one of the earliest and most beloved skins in the game, known for its simple yet stylish design. Its unique laser-like shooting sound and smooth reload animation make it extremely satisfying to use. Many players consider it a “classic” top-tier skin.

#10

Champions 2021 Vandal

About

The Champions 2021 Vandal is a limited-edition esports skin that glows brighter as you get more kills. Its rarity and prestige make it highly valued in the community, especially among players who like exclusive items.
